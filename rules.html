Senior Assassination 2024 - Rules

Senior Assassination is a game played by high school seniors. The objective is to "assassinate" other players by "eliminating" them from the game using water guns or similar devices. Here are the rules:

  1. Only seniors are allowed to play.
  2. Some will have the same person. First one to get the kill gets a point.
  3. Players must be registered with the game organizer aka Hein.
  4. Eliminations may be made on school grounds or out of school
  5. Eliminations may not be made during class time or while a player is engaged in a school activity.
  6. Players may not carry water guns or similar devices on school grounds unless they are engaged in the game.
  7. Players must use only water guns or similar devices that are easily distinguishable from real weapons.
  8. Players may not use any substance other than water in their water guns.
  9. Eliminations may only be made by shooting another player with water.
  10. Players may not physically touch or tackle another player.
  11. Eliminated must be recored to be counted as a kill.
  12. The game organizer has final say in any disputes.
